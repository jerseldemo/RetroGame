About DOOM

DOOM, developed by id Software and released in 1993, is often credited as one of the most influential video games ever created. It was a pioneering first-person shooter (FPS) that set the standard for many future games in the genre.

Players take on the role of a space marine who must battle his way through hordes of demons and monsters, all while navigating the corridors of hellish environments. DOOM's fast-paced action, immersive 3D environments, and intense combat helped make it an instant classic.

DOOM's multiplayer mode allowed players to engage in deathmatches, which was groundbreaking at the time. This multiplayer feature played a significant role in establishing online gaming as a major component of the industry.
