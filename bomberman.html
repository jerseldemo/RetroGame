About BOMBERMAN

BOMBERMAN, developed by Hudson Soft and released in 1983, is a strategic action game that involves players navigating a grid-based maze, planting bombs, and avoiding enemies. The objective is to destroy walls and defeat enemies with the bombs while avoiding being caught in their own explosions.

The game is known for its simple yet addictive gameplay, where players need to think quickly and strategically to survive. In multiplayer modes, players can compete against each other to be the last one standing. Over the years, BOMBERMAN has spawned numerous sequels and variations, but its roots in the 1983 arcade game remain beloved by fans of retro gaming.
