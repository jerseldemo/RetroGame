About CONTRA

CONTRA, developed by Konami and released in 1987, is one of the most iconic action games of the 1980s. The game was a standout for its cooperative two-player gameplay, where players could team up to defeat the Red Falcon organization and save the world from an alien invasion.

The game features a side-scrolling format, where players move through various environments, including jungles, military bases, and alien-infested areas. Players are armed with a variety of weapons, and the game features power-ups that increase their firepower or grant additional lives.

The game's difficulty is legendary, and its tight, fast-paced action kept players on the edge of their seats. Its most famous feature is the infamous "Konami Code," a cheat that grants players 30 extra lives. This code became a part of gaming culture and is still remembered today.

CONTRA was revolutionary for its time, with graphics and gameplay that pushed the limits of what was possible on arcade machines. It has been ported to many systems over the years, and its influence can still be seen in many modern action games. Today, CONTRA is remembered as one of the greatest games ever created, a true classic in the gaming world.
