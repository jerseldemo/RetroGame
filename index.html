Retro video games

The era of retro video games is a cherished chapter in gaming history, a time when 8-bit and 16-bit consoles like the **NES**, **Sega Genesis**, and **Super Nintendo** defined our childhoods. The pixelated graphics and simple sounds might seem primitive now, but they opened doors to fantastical worlds that fueled our imaginations. Heroes like **Mario**, **Sonic**, and **Link** led us on epic adventures, while games like **Pac-Man** and **Street Fighter II** became legendary.
