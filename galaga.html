About GALAGA

GALAGA, released by Namco in 1986, is one of the most iconic shoot-'em-up games of all time. In this game, players control a spaceship at the bottom of the screen, shooting waves of alien enemies descending from the top of the screen.

The gameplay is simple but thrilling: players must avoid enemy fire while attempting to shoot down the alien ships. Some enemy ships can capture the player's spaceship, and it's up to the player to rescue it and continue the battle.

GALAGA's challenging gameplay, combined with its colorful graphics and catchy sound effects, has made it a classic in the arcade genre. The game is known for its high score challenges, encouraging players to keep playing to beat their best score.

GALAGA has seen many sequels and updates over the years, but the 1986 version remains one of the most beloved retro games of all time, still celebrated by fans of arcade shooters.
